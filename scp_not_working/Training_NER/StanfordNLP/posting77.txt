Position Description.

Responsible for ensuring all merchandise prices are displayed correctly by performing price reviews, and maintaining pricing signage inside and outside of the store. Includes auditing and reviewing special order books, ordering any missing items or new inserts, and reviewing and completing all system tasks. Also responsible for greeting and acknowledging all customers in a friendly, professional manner and providing quick, responsive customer service.

Job Requirements.

Requires morning, afternoon, and evening availability any day of the week. Physical ability to move large, bulky and/or heavy merchandise. Physical ability to perform tasks that may require prolonged standing, sitting, and other activities necessary to perform job duties.

Minimum Qualifications.

Ability to read, write, and perform basic arithmetic (addition, subtraction).

Preferred Qualifications.

6 months experience with price integrity/audit.

6 months experience with Lowe's.
