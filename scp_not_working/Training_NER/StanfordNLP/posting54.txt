Position Summary.

The principle purpose of the Sales Support Associate is to assist customers in selection, demonstration, and purchase of product including special orders and installations. The Sales Support Associate will also participate in the safe, accurate and efficient receiving, unloading, dispersion and stocking of inbound freight to prepare the store for daily customer activity.

Minimum Qualifications.

 High School Diploma.

Preferred Qualifications.

 1 year of experience in retail store freight flow logistics.
 Experience in customer service role.
 Experience working in a fast paced, dynamic retail environment.
