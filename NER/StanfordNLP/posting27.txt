DESCRIPTION

Responsible for creating a workroom environment that promotes production processes, with a focus on quality finished garments. Provides support for relationships among Fitters, Sales Associates, and Customers. This position manages the Fur Workroom and Storage Facility at the West Coast Service Center. Center provides storage, cleaning, glazing, repairs and remodels to customer owned furs within the region. Manager coordinates all services with the twelve full-line stores and their fur customers. Manager has full P&L responsibility ensuring profit objectives are met annually. Manager oversees a staff including a coordinator, clerical, and artisans.
