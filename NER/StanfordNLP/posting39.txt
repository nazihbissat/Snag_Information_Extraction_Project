DESCRIPTION

Responsible for partnering with sales associates to satisfy customers' expectations for fit, alterations, re-sizing and re-styling of all merchandise retailed in a Neiman Marcus store.

  - Customer Focus
  - Presentation Skills
  - Managing and Measuring Work
