Job Description

Education: High school diploma or general education degree (GED) preferred

Work Experiences:

  - At least one years of retail experience in a complex, large format store
  - In-direct support of people and processes to drive operational excellence and expected sales targets

Skills:

  - Excellent customer service orientation
  - Effective problem solving and communication with customers and team members
  - Operate and coach others on use of related equipment, symbol and telephone
  - Reads, interprets and follows documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, plan-o-grams and procedure manuals.
  - Writes routine reports and correspondence
  - Applies common sense understanding to carry out instructions furnished in written, oral or diagram form
  - Working knowledge of inventory software, order processing systems, and Microsoft Office products such as Word and Excel

Responsibilities:

  - Key contributor on both the Operations and Loss Prevention teams in minimizing shrink through visibility of high theft categories and better inventory controls.
  - Drives customer service through cross functional partnerships and by ensuring compliance to Academy's established sales, safety and security procedures and practices.
  - Documents and maintains records of incidents relating to loss prevention and safety issues.
  - Monitors and accurately reports adjustments and discrepancies to store technology to ensure company security standards are maintained.
  - Partners with Compliance team to execute product recalls and related processes.
  - Attends and participate in all required trainings and meetings.
  - Provides continual feedback on processes and procedures; suggest methods to improve department operations, efficiency and service to both internal and external customers.
  - Reads, understands and follows company policies, procedures and safety rules.
  - Adhere to company work hours, policies, procedures and rules governing professional staff behavior.
  - Duties may change and team members may be required to perform other duties or roles as assigned.

Physical Requirements & Attendance

  - Ability to work a flexible schedule that includes nights, weekends, and holidays as needed.
  - Constantly required to hear, stand, handle, use fingers, grasp, and use repetitive motions (hand/wrist/fingers) and feel.
  - Frequently required to walk, reach, and talk.
  - Occasionally required to sit, climb, balance, stoop, kneel, crouch, push and pull.
  - Constantly lift up to 10 pounds and occasionally lift 11 to 30 pounds.
  - Specific vision abilities required by this role include close vision, distance vision, peripheral vision, depth perception and ability to adjust focus.
